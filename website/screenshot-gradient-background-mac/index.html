From bare capture to polished image

A raw screenshot pasted into a tweet, a slide, or a landing page looks flat. A gradient backdrop with breathing room and a shadow makes it look intentional. macshot's Beautify does exactly that without sending your image to a website.

What you can adjust

  • Gradient style - choose from 30 presets, including mesh gradients on macOS 15+.
  • Padding - how much space sits around the screenshot.
  • Rounded corners - soften the screenshot's edges.
  • Shadow - lift it off the background with a drop shadow.

Offline by design

Most "beautify screenshot" tools are websites you upload to. macshot renders the gradient on your Mac, so it's instant, works without a connection, and your screenshot never leaves the machine.

Pairs with the rest of macshot

Beautify sits alongside the full annotation toolkit - arrows, text, numbered steps, blur - and works great with background removal when you want a clean subject on a colorful backdrop.

Free, native, and open source

macshot is a native Swift and AppKit app, free and open source under the GPLv3 license, with no subscription, account, or watermark.

Common questions

How do I put a gradient background behind a screenshot on Mac?

macshot's Beautify feature wraps your screenshot in a gradient backdrop with padding, rounded corners, and a soft shadow. Pick from 30 styles, adjust the padding and corner radius, then copy or save the result.

Does it have mesh gradients?

Yes. macshot includes 30 background styles, with mesh gradients available on macOS 15 and later for a richer, multi-color look.

Does it upload my screenshot anywhere?

No. Beautify renders entirely on your Mac. Unlike web-based screenshot beautifiers, nothing is uploaded.

Is macshot free?

Yes. macshot is free and open source under the GPLv3 license, with no subscription or account.
